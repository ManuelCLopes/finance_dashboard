How It Works

This finance dashboard allows you to securely upload your financial data. Simply scan the QR code, and the dashboard will generate detailed charts that show your expenses, income, and investments.

FAQ

How do I upload my financial data to the web dashboard?

Upload your financial data by scanning the QR code generated by the Finance Tracker mobile app. Your data will be visualized in real-time on the web interface.

What types of charts can I view on the web dashboard?

You can view bar charts, pie charts, line charts, and stacked bar charts. These charts help you visualize your expenses, income, and investments.

How secure is the data upload process?

Data is securely transferred via encryption, ensuring that your financial information remains private and protected.

Does the web dashboard support multiple devices?

Yes, the web dashboard is accessible on any device with a browser, and it's fully responsive for desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
